The Freedom Express Challenge

Freedom Express! In the name of efficiency, Super Earth commands only the swiftest of its elite to answer this call. The enemy grows bolder by the second, and hesitation is treason. Your mission: land, execute the primary objective with ruthless precision, and extract before the enemy knows what hit them. This is not a test of endurance â€” it's a test of speed, clarity, and unshakable devotion to the cause. Glory to the fast. Glory to Super Earth.

Complete 3 operations on difficulty 10 (Super Helldive) as fast as possible using the assigned stratagem and boosters. Points are earned by time remaining and extracting with the High Value Item. Deaths deduct points from your score.

Your score per mission is determined by 3 factors:

  • % Mission Time Remaining
    • 1% = +1 point
  • The High Value Item
    • Extract with HVI = +10 points
  • Deaths
    • 1 Death = -1 points

After each successfully completed mission, press the green "Mission Complete" button and follow the prompts. Your running score will be updated and your progress will be automatically saved.

The run ends when you fail a mission. Press the red "Mission Failed" button to start a new run. You will be given a prompt to confirm.

Your progress is saved in your browser's local storage. If you clear your browser's local storage, your progress will be lost.
You can download your save file and upload it to a different device with the Save Data Management menu (the blue floppy disk button in the upper right), change the name of your run, and delete any of your save data in this menu as well.

  • Q: Who was this challenge made for?

    A: This challenge was made for veteran players who are looking to spice up their Super Helldive operations with speedrun elements and a little bit of RNG.

  • Q: How does my squad use the boosters?

    A: Every player will see a random set of four boosters available to them every mission. Every player can choose one of the four boosters on their screen. Coordinate with your team to bring the optimal set of boosters for a given mission.

  • Q: How many squadmates do I need to run this challenge?

    A: This challenge is equally viable for solo players or a squad of participating divers.

  • Q: Can we share/trade equipment?

    A: YES. Sharing is caring.
