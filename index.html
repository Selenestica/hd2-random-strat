  • Q: Who is this app for?

    A: This web app was made for Helldivers who have unlocked all stratagems (and ideally, equipment) and are looking for a fun way to spice up their dives.

  • Q: What if I don't want to bring multiple support weapon/backpack stratagems?

    A: In the Options menu, in the Randomizer Settings tab, you will be able to customize the randomizer.

  • Q: What if I roll a booster, but a teammate has already chosen it?

    A: Feel free to choose a different booster than the one you have rolled. I don't make the rules, I just make the tools!

  • Q: What you be adding a randomizer for specific armor sets?

    A: Seeing as how many armor sets are only available in the Superstore, I won't be adding a randomizer that rolls for specific armor sets.

  • Q: Why don't you run ads on this site?

    A: That wouldn't be very democratic, now would it?
