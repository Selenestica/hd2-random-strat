    • Q: Who is this app for?

      A: This web app was made for Helldivers who are seeking a challenge, either solo or with a squad.

    • Q: What if I want to have multiple challenge runs at the same time?

      A: You can start a new challenge at any time, and this website will save your progress from your previous runs automatically for every challenge.

    • Q: Why don't you run ads on this site?

      A: That wouldn't be very democratic, now would it?
