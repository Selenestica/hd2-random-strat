Info

  • Q: Who is this app for?

    A: This web app was made for Helldivers who are seeking a challenge, either solo or with a squad.

  • Q: What if I want to have multiple challenge runs at the same time?

    A: You can start a new challenge at any time, and this website will save your progress from your previous runs automatically.

  • Q: Why don't you run ads on this site?

    A: That wouldn't be very democratic, now would it?

  • Huge thanks to u/TylerJohnsonDaGOAT for writing up the attributes for stratagems and equipment, and for letting me bounce ideas off of them. Tons of drudgery was avoided because of their contributions. Thanks!
  • I came across this idea while browsing Reddit. After a quick Google search, I found that someone had already made a web app for this challenge (very cool). However, I had my own ideas regarding layout, more features, etc, and so here we are!
