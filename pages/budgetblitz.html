The Budget Blitz Challenge

“The price of liberty is vigilance... and a balanced ledger.” As the galaxy grows ever more perilous, and as enemies multiply like interest on unpaid debts, the Super Earth High Command has instituted a Budget Blitz: a bold new initiative that forges the might of our military with the unyielding strength of economic stewardship. In these trying times, resources are finite, but our resolve is infinite. Each mission’s outcome is no longer measured solely by enemy casualties or territories secured — but by the credits accrued. Victory must pay its own way.

“The strength of our democracy is built on the sacrifices of our soldiers—and the wisdom of our accountants. Under a Budget Blitz, every credit spent is a vote for freedom. Every star earned is a promise kept. Helldivers, carry the torch of liberty... but keep an eye on the ledger.”

Complete all missions in one operation on each difficulty, starting on level 5 and moving up one difficulty as you go. The amount of stars earned after each mission corresponds to how many credits you will earn. Your goal is to complete a level 10 difficulty operation.

After each mission, press the 'Mission Complete' button and input the number of stars you earned. You will earn credits for each star awarded at the end of every mission.

In higher difficulties, extra credits can be earned by extracting with any Super Samples and High-Value Items found.

Press the 'Shop' button to view purchaseable items. Any item purchased will only be usable for the amount of missions you purchased it for. After you use the item for the amount of missions that you purchased it for, it will dissapear from your inventory and the shop, and you will not be able to use or purchase it again, so stratagize accordingly.

For example: If I purchased the Quasar Cannon to be used for three missions, after those two missions I will not be able to purchase or use it again for the rest of the challenge.

Alternatively, you can press the 'Surplus' button to roll a random item at a discount that will be usable for a minimum of two missions. These items will be chosen at random among the list of available items in the shop.

If your squad fails a mission, you must start a new operation on the difficulty that you failed to complete. You will not lose credits, but item usage will still be counted. Press the "Mission Failed" button to reset the operation.

Your progress is saved in your browser's local storage. If you clear your browser's local storage, your progress will be lost. If you want to start a new run, you may do so by pressing the yellow "Restart" button. Your run will be saved and accessible in the save menu.
