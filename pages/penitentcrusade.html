Background

Your Helldivers faction failed to defend Moradesh, and High Command has ordered you and your compatriots to undergo a Penitent Crusade in order to atone for your faction's failure.

The Penitent Crusade

Your squad will begin the crusade with a Medium (Level 3) difficulty operation, with Super Earth's outdated and surplus equipment. After completing each mission in an operation, your squad will begin a new operation at the next highest difficulty level. Your squad's goal is to complete an operation at the highest difficulty level, Super Helldive (Level 10). Only then will your Helldivers faction be redeemed. You may start this crusade on any front.

Earning Rewards

In order to appease High Command and be rewarded for your efforts, you must complete each mission with the maximum amount of stars. After a mission is completed with maximum stars, press the "Mission Complete" button. You may choose one of the three items presented to you.
NOTE: High-tier equipment and stratagems are more likely to appear as you progress through your crusade.

Using Your Equipment

The equipment you see in the background is what you currently have unlocked. Any items you earn through mission rewards will appear there as well. While you're in-mission, you may not use any stratagems that are available for free (e.g. experimental weapons testing, arsenal augmentations, etc). Only the stratagems you have unlocked and objective-specific stratagems (e.g. Hellbomb, SSSD, SEAF Artillery, SOS, etc.) may be used. However, you're free to pick up and use any stratagems found in your environment (such as those that can be looted at Points of Interest).

Failure

If your squad fails a mission, you must start a new operation on the difficulty that you failed to complete. Furthermore, you must choose an item to be removed from your inventory. Press the "Mission Failed" button, then choose an item to be taken from you.

Saving Your Progress

Your rewards are saved in your browser's local storage. If you clear your browser's local storage, your progress will be lost. If you want to clear your progress, you may do so by pressing the "Restart" button. This will clear your progress and restart the game. You will be prompted to confirm this action.
