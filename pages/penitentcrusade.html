Background

Your Helldivers faction failed to defend Moradesh, and High Command has ordered you and your compatriots to undergo a Penitent Crusade in order to atone for your faction's failure.

The Penitent Crusade

Your squad will begin the crusade with a Medium (Level 3) difficulty operation, with Super Earth's outdated and surplus equipment. After each successful operation, your squad will begin a new operation at the next highest difficulty level. Your squad's goal is to complete an operation at the highest difficulty level, Super Helldive (Level 10). Only then will your Helldivers faction be redeemed.

Earning Rewards

After each successful mission, each squad member will be rewarded with one of the following items: a Primary weapon, a Stratagem, or a Booster. Press the "Mission Complete" button, then choose your reward type. Furthermore, if your squad earns the maximum amount of stars on the mission, each squad member may choose between a random secondary, throwable, or armor passive. For this, press the "Max Stars" button, and choose your reward type.

Using Your Equipment

The equipment you see in the background is what you currently have unlocked, and you're free to use. Any items you earn through mission rewards will appear there as well. While you're in-mission, you may not use any stratagems that are available for free (e.g. experimental weapons testing, etc.), unless it is a mission-specific stratagem (e.g. Hellbomb, SSSD, SEAF Artillery, etc.). However, you're free to pick up any weapons found in your environment.
