  • Q: Who is this app for?

    A: This web app was made for Helldivers who are looking for a fun way to spice up their dives.

  • Q: What if I don't have some of the items in a warbond/the superstore/from the Pre-Order Bonus?

    A: You can toggle these items off in the Options menu. If you don't have the items, you won't roll them.

  • Q: What if I don't want to bring multiple support weapon/backpack stratagems?

    A: In the Options menu, in the Randomizer Settings tab, you will be able to customize the randomizer.

  • Q: What if I roll a booster, but a teammate has already chosen it?

    A: You can reroll just the booster by clicking on the booster icon. Or, feel free to choose a different booster than the one you have rolled. I don't make the rules, I just make the tools!

  • Q: Why don't you run ads on this site?

    A: The intention behind making this site was to provide maximum value to Helldivers everywhere. The majority of my code is browser-based, and I only pay out-of-pocket for the icons, build time, and hosting (I use AWS). Costs are relatively small, but if you would like to reward me for my efforts, feel free to buy me a coffee using the link at the top of the page!

  • Q: Can I contribute with time instead of money?

    A: Please! Feel free to join the discord and hit me up if you have any ideas/suggestions, or run into any bugs. If you're a coder, I can definitely use your help with upcoming features.
