    The Special Operations Challenge

    In the wake of growing threats across the galactic frontier, Super Earth’s High Command has authorized the deployment of elite operatives under the Specialist Challenge Initiative. Each Helldiver is assigned a combat role and dispatched to hostile planets under strict operational parameters. These missions are more than just war—they're a proving ground. Those who survive not only bring liberty to alien worlds but also gain clearance to deploy with newly de-classified specialist loadouts. One role at a time, Helldivers climb the ranks, mastering tools of democracy previously locked behind bureaucratic red tape and explosive field tests. Glory awaits. Failure is treason.

    Specialists aren’t born. They’re forged—in fire, gas, plasma, and paperwork

    Complete an operation on the given planet using the assigned specialist loadout, and fulfilling all special operation objectives, in order to unlock new specialists. There are over a dozen specialists to unlock, with more specialists to be added in the future as more content is added to the game.

    Operations must be of difficulty 8 or higher. Each operation will have a list of 3 additional objectives that must be completed in order to unlock a new specialist afterward.

    Upon mission success, click the 'Mission Complete' button, and input the progress you made toward fulfilling your operation's objectives in the after-mission report.

    If you fail a mission, click the 'Mission Failed' button. You will start a new operation with new objectives, on a different planet.

    After each successfully completed special operation, you will be automatically assigned a new and unlocked specialist. You may choose a previously unlocked specialist to play, but progress will not be made toward unlocking a new specialist.

    In other words, you must complete an operation with your newest unlocked specialist () in order to unlock the next specialist.

    While you're planetside, you may share equipment with your squadmates, and you are allowed to pick up new equipment from your environment.

    A planet will be assigned to you along with a set of operational objectives, as long as you weren't previously in the middle of an operation.

    If you're part of a squad and you need to go to a different planet, open the planet list and choose the planet you want. Only planets that are able to be liberated or defended will show up on the list. A set of operational objectives will be given to you, and you will lose any operational progress on any other planet, if any.

    NOTE: Operational objectives on a planet may vary from player to player. Work together to ensure everyone in your squad meets their objective requirements.

    Your progress is saved in your browser's local storage. If you clear your browser's local storage, your progress will be lost.
    You can download your save file and upload it to a different device with the Save Data Management menu. Uploading a save file will overwrite any previous saved data for this challenge.
