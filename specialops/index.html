    The Special Operations Challenge

    In the wake of growing threats across the galactic frontier, Super Earth High Command has authorized the deployment of elite operatives. Each Helldiver is assigned a combat role and dispatched to hostile planets under strict operational parameters. These missions are more than just war—they're a proving ground. Those who survive not only bring liberty to alien worlds but also gain clearance to deploy with newly de-classified specialist loadouts. One role at a time, Helldivers climb the ranks, mastering tools of democracy previously locked behind bureaucratic red tape and explosive field tests. Glory awaits. Failure is treason.

    Specialists aren’t born. They’re forged—in fire, gas, plasma, and paperwork

    Complete an operation on the given planet using the assigned specialist loadout, and fulfilling all operation goals, in order to unlock new specialist loadouts. There are many specialist loadouts to unlock, with more specialist loadouts to be added in the future as more content is added to the game.

    Operations must be on difficulty 10 (Super Helldive). Each operation will have 2 goals that must be completed in order to progress.

    Upon mission success, click the 'Mission Complete' button, and input the progress you made toward fulfilling your operation's goals in the fields provided.

    If you fail a mission, click the 'Mission Failed' button. You will be given a new operation with new goals, on a different planet.

    After each successfully completed special operation, you will be automatically assigned a new specialist loadout. You may choose a previously unlocked loadout to play, but progress will not be made toward unlocking a new specialist.

    In other words, you must complete an operation with your latest unlocked specialist () in order to unlock the next specialist.

    While you're planetside, you may share equipment with your squadmates. You're allowed to pick up equipment from your environment as well.

    A planet will be assigned to you along with a set of operation goals, as long as you weren't previously in the middle of an operation.

    If you're part of a squad and you need to go to a different planet, open the planet list ( ) and choose the planet you want. Only planets that are able to be liberated or defended will show up on the list. A new set of operation goals will be given to you, and you will lose your progress on your previous planet.

    NOTE: Operation goals on a planet may vary from player to player. Work together to ensure everyone in your squad meets their goals.

    Your progress is saved in your browser's local storage. If you clear your browser's local storage, your progress will be lost.
    You can download your save file and upload it to a different device with the Save Data Management menu. Uploading a save file will overwrite any previous saved data for this challenge.

    • Q: Who was this challenge made for?

      A: This challenge was made for veteran players who are looking to spice up their Super Helldive operations with rogue-like elements, themed loadouts, and roleplay.

    • Q: How many squadmates do I need to run this challenge?

      A: This challenge is equally viable for solo players, a squad of friends, or a squad of randoms as you can progress at your own pace.

    • Q: What are points for and how are they earned?

      A: Points are there for bragging rights, and do not affect rewards or RNG in any way.

      For most objectives, 5 points are earned if the objective is accomplished in the first mission of an operation, 3 points are earned for accomplishing an objective in the second mission of an operation, and 1 point is earned for accomplishing an objective int he last mission of an operation.

      For objectives that are impossible to accomplish in the first mission of an operation, 5 points are given for accomplishing the objective at the end of the operation.

    • Q: What if the planet I had an operation on is no longer available?

      A: If you or your squadmates can no longer access that planet in-game, choose a different planet from the planet list ( ). This list updates every ten minutes or so.

    • Q: Why didn't I unlock a new specialist after I completed an operation?

      A: You may not have met the operational objectives, or you may not have the correct specialist loadout equipped.

    • Q: How do I know which specialist I will unlock next?

      A: Your next unlocked specialist will be chosen at random amongst the list of specialists you have yet to unlock.

    Challenge Settings

    Toggle the warbonds you have obtained here, as well as Superstore and Pre-Order bonus items. This will affect the specialist loadouts you have available to unlock.
    For the best experience, toggle warbonds before you start an operation.

    NOTE: If your current loadout is affected by your selection, a new loadout will be unlocked for you.
