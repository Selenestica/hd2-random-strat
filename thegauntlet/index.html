    The Gauntlet Challenge

    During the First Galactic War the famed Rabble Rousers, the group that inspired the creation of the Mad Bastards, completed a series of harrowing jumps across the galaxy. Documented in Vera Holt's war journal, the Rabble Rousers were chasing a rogue transmission that was bouncing through the relays all across the galaxy.

    Supplies grew shorter. Munitions stockpiles dwindled. Time was running out. But in the end, the Rabble Rousers were successful.

    In the honored spirit of this heroic feat, the Hell Admiral has established The Gauntlet as a means of testing the most determined of Mad Bastards. Those who follow in the footsteps of the honored Rabble Rousers come through the other side changed, born anew in the radiance of Democracy. Wielders of Liberty's fire and Bane to Tyranny everywhere.

    The Gauntlet is a series of missions with increasingly difficult constraints, across multiple enemy factions. Completing one 40 minute mission at each difficulty, starting from difficulty 5 and ending at difficulty 10.
    The challenge can be undertaken with any amount of players, including solo.

    With each successful mission, the challenge increases not only by the difficulty itself, but with additional constraints as well.

    Upon mission success, click the 'Mission Complete' button, and provide the information required.

    If you fail a mission, click the 'Mission Failed' button.

    Your progress is saved in your browser's local storage. If you clear your browser's local storage, your progress will be lost.
    You can download your save file and upload it to a different device with the Save Data Management menu. Uploading a save file will overwrite any previous saved data for this challenge.

    • Q: Who was this challenge made for?

      A: This challenge was made for veteran players who are looking to spice up their Super Helldive operations with rogue-like elements, themed loadouts, and roleplay.

    • Q: How do boons work for specialists?

      A: Each specialist has it's own boons (bonuses). After you've selected your specialist you will be prompted to apply your squadmates' specialists, so that their boons can be added to your total resources.

      If you're solo, you can simply check the "Solo" option and you will never see the menu again.

    • Q: How many squadmates do I need to run this challenge?

      A: This challenge is equally viable for solo playersor a squad of friends who are also running the challenge.

    • Q: Can I change classes?

      A: If you fail a mission, for any reason, you may choose another class.
