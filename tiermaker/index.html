    Tier List Maker

    • Q: I made a tier list a while back, but now it's gone. What happened?

      A: Significant changes have been made to the page as of 8 January 2026, adding new features. Unfortunately, this meant that I had to change the way data is saved, and your old data may have been lost. Please feel free to reach out to me on Discord and I will try to recover your old data!

    • Q: Can I save my tier list?

      A: Yes! Your lists, like everything else on this site, is saved in your browsers local storage. You can even have multiple lists saved and switch between them any time.

    • Q: Can I share my tier list with others?

      A: Yes! Simply click the download button next to the save file that you want to share. This will download a text file that others can upload in their app.

    • Q: Why don't you run ads on this site?

      A: The intention behind making this site was to provide maximum value to Helldivers everywhere. The majority of my code is browser-based, and I only pay out-of-pocket for the domain, build time, and hosting (I use AWS). Costs are relatively small, but if you would like to reward me for my efforts, feel free to buy me a coffee using the link at the top of the page!

    • Q: Can I contribute with time instead of money?

      A: Please! Feel free to join the discord and hit me up if you have any ideas/suggestions, or run into any bugs. If you're a coder, I can definitely use your help with upcoming features.
